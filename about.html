About

Hi, I'm Luke McConnell. I am a Software Engineer currently working as a Computer Scientist on space ground systems software for the Air Force Civilian Service. I work in a special group of engineers functioning as government contractors for the U.S. Space Force. I've held this position since graduating from the University of Colorado Boulder with a B.S. Computer Science in August of 2020.
I am proud to have served 6 years in the U.S. Navy as a Nuclear Machinist's Mate. I gained a vast experience in nuclear engineering operations and maintenance. While assigned to an aircraft carrier, I was lucky enough to live in Japan for 3 years and travel to 11 countries. After 4 deployments, my contract came to a close and I was honorably discharged.
My academic and professional evolution from Nuclear Operations to Computer Science has been transformative. I enjoy leveraging my diverse skills to solve problems of all kinds. Below is my resume. Thanks for visiting my site!

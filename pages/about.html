About Me

Hi, I'm Luke McConnell. I am a B.S. Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder, graduating in August of 2020. I enjoy computer programing, computer animation and iterative design. I most frequently use Python and C++ programming languages. I have had a successful undergraduate career in classes like Data Structures, Algorithms, Robotics, Data Science, and Artifical Intelligence. I have also been on successful team projects that have produced deliverables greater than any individual. I have completed computer-related projects in a variety of langauges for wide array of purposes. Here are a few things I've done:

  • Built 2 computers from indivudual parts sourced on the Internet. No kits, no build packages.
  • Used code to actuate large stepper motors, linear actuators, and electric locks in sequence.
  • Developed working map-based Android App with user authentication.
  • Wrote a program to take user input and sift through large amounts of geoJSON data to create an HTML Folium map output.
  • Created numerous small task Bash and Python scripts for automating processes and making API calls.

I am also a U.S. Navy Veteran. I served 6 years as a Nuclear Machinist's Mate. I was forward deployed out of Yokosuka, Japan for 3 years. During this time I visited Australia, South Korea, Hong Kong, Thailand, Singapore, Cambodia, Guam, Peru, Chile, Brazil, and the Phillipines. I gained a vast experience in nuclear engineering operations and maintenance. After 4 deployments, my contract came to a close and I was honorably discharged. I am working to showcase my experiences and qualifications on this online portfolio. My current resume is posted below. Thanks for visting!
